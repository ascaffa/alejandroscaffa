Crème de la crème de la mediocrite

The title 14/4/2016

The title comes from my PhD advisor's PhD advisor, my academic grandfather. For those who don't know the expression, albeit only the beginning is actually an expression, it means the best of the best. My PI, someone who I truly admire, said this to me once, on a whim, when I was being optimistic while nothing was working at the laboratory. Young and naive, although this was just a few months ago, I considered that my work was good work regardless of the scientific outcome. But, as I come to understand, you might be Crème de la crème, but unless the results are positive, you're only Crème de la crème de la mediocrite.
