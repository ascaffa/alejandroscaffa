In a nutshell...

I'm in my last year of PhD at the of Department Molecular Pharmacology and Physiology, at Brown University. My advisor is Phyllis Dennery, MD, and under her mentorship I research Bronchopulmonary Displasia (BPD), metabolism, and senescence. See my research.

I have a strong background in genetics, CRISPR, and biochemistry.

I'm curious, driven, a team player, and I'm passionate about innovative science. Such as medical devices, high throughput drug discovery and development, and data science. I also find business development and startup exciting.

I have a biochemistry BA from Grinnell College in Iowa, and MA in molecular pharmacology and physiology from Brown University.

I'm open to job offers and eligible to work in USA, Argentina, Brazil, and Europe.
